Our approach

Quantitative investing made accessible

Hidden Point Capital is a quantitative investment management company focused on managing a long/short equity portfolio. We are dedicated to producing exceptional returns for our investors by strictly adhering to quantitative and statistical methods and risk management techniques.

The fund invests in individual equities and equity-related instruments of companies in global developed markets, focused primarily on the United States. The fund uses quantitative investing models and approaches exclusively. The fund's strategy combines multiple independent sources of potential returns.

We focus on key aspects of the portfolio formation process which is supported by a significant body of academic research and empirical analysis.

The process

Key components of a rigorous process

  1. Step 01

    Universe selection

    We screen roughly 3,000 U.S. equities down to the most liquid, investable names.

  2. Step 02

    Multi-factor analysis

    Fundamental, technical, and sentiment signals are combined through adaptive machine-learning models.

  3. Step 03

    Sector ranking

    Candidates are scored on a sector-relative basis to isolate genuine alpha.

  4. Step 04

    Regime-based exposure

    Tactical exposure adjusts to the prevailing market and volatility regime.

  5. Step 05

    Position sizing

    Capital concentrates in the highest-conviction names, beta-normalized and risk-capped.

  6. Step 06

    Continuous rebalancing

    The portfolio is kept in tune on a disciplined weekly cadence.

  7. Step 07

    Dynamic hedging

    A beta-targeted index hedge scales with the regime to manage drawdowns.

We use machine learning and statistical tools together with massive amounts of pricing data, fundamental company information and state of the art computational resources to continuously adapt the portfolio to the current market conditions.

This approach results in a portfolio of positions that in the long run will provide downside protection under adverse market conditions while providing ample upside participation during periods of positive equity market returns.
