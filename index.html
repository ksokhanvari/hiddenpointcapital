Overview

A portfolio of long-hedged equities

The fund invests in individual equities and equity-related instruments of companies in the global developed markets, focused primarily on the United States.

We use quantitative investing models exclusively to combine multiple independent sources of a potential return to achieve the fund's objectives. Our strategy seeks to maximize expected return subject to a targeted portfolio volatility range. It uses a dynamic allocation methodology that takes into account market volatility, asset volatility, and underlying market momentum while building the portfolio ground up from a set of proprietary factor analysis models.

The result is a portfolio of positions that in the long run aims to significantly outperform its benchmarks while providing low beta exposure to markets and downside protection under adverse market conditions.
